How to read this graph

  • ⬤Node size reflects number of connections — larger nodes are more central to the story.
  • 🎨Node colour indicates category: Person, Organisation, Event, Place, Legal, Finding, Outcome, or Digital.
  • →Arrows show directed relationships between entities (e.g. "charged under", "caused", "organised").
  • 👆Click a node to open its detail panel with full description and all connections.
  • ✥Drag nodes to reposition them and untangle the graph.
  • 🔍Scroll or pinch to zoom in and out of the graph.
  • ⬚Filter buttons in the top bar show or hide entire categories.
  • 🔎Search fades out any node whose label or description doesn't match.
